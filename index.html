01 — ABOUT

A little about me

Hello! I'm Praya Pokharel, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) specializing in Network Technology and Cybersecurity at Texas College of Management & IT in Kathmandu. I split my time between building things for the web and pulling them apart to see how they break.

My interest in security didn't start with one big moment — it grew out of tinkering with Linux, poking around network configurations, and slowly realizing that the more interesting question in tech usually isn't how something works, but how it fails. That curiosity is what pulled me toward penetration testing, CTFs, and the endpoint security and IS audit modules alongside my regular coursework.

On the development side, I enjoy the other half of that equation: actually building the systems I'm learning to secure. Working across HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python keeps me grounded in how real applications are structured, which makes the security side click faster — it's easier to defend something once you've built it yourself.

Right now that means splitting my time between formal coursework, hands-on labs on TryHackMe, and personal projects — with the long-term goal of working somewhere at the intersection of network infrastructure and security operations.
